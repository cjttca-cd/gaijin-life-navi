# マイナ保険証: 2024 Health Insurance Card System Reform Guide

⚠️ **Important Notice**: Japan's traditional health insurance card (旧保険証) became **fully invalid on December 1, 2025**. From December 2, 2025 onward, the old insurance card can no longer be used; you will need to use マイナ保険証 or 資格確認書 when visiting medical facilities.

---

## System Change Timeline

| Milestone | Change |
|---------|---------|
| December 2, 2024 | Issuance of new 旧保険証 discontinued |
| Through December 1, 2025 | Transition period — existing 旧保険証 could continue to be used within their validity period (immediately invalid upon insurance change due to job change or relocation) |
| December 2, 2025 | **All 旧保険証 become invalid**; only マイナ保険証 or 資格確認書 are valid |

## What You Need at Medical Facilities (From December 2, 2025)

**Option A: Using マイナ保険証 (Recommended)**

- マイナンバーカード (with health insurance card usage registration completed)
- Face authentication card reader at the medical facility → facial recognition or 4-digit PIN → registration complete
- Benefits: past medical records shared, 高額療養費 counter limit automatically applied, no re-registration required after job change

**Option B: Without マイナ保険証 → 資格確認書**

- Present 資格確認書 at the reception desk; usage is the same as the old insurance card
- Applicable to: those who have not applied for a マイナンバーカード, those who have not yet completed usage registration, and those whose digital certificate has expired
- **No application required, free of charge**; automatically issued by your insurer, valid for up to 5 years

**Option C: In the Event of Equipment Failure or Power Outage**

- You can display your eligibility information screen via マイナポータル, or fill in a 資格申立書
- You will not be required to pay the full amount out-of-pocket (10 割負担にはならない)

## How to Register for マイナ保険証 Usage (Free)

The following three methods are available for registration:

1. **At a medical facility reception desk**: register for the first time using the face authentication card reader
2. **マイナポータル** (official app): Menu → 健康・医療 → 健康保険証の利用申込
3. **セブン銀行 ATM**: follow the on-screen instructions

From November 2025, smartphones can also be used as マイナ保険証 (iPhone via Apple Wallet, Android via the smartphone digital certificate), limited to compatible medical facilities.

## Notes for Foreign Residents

- Once 住民登録 is complete, a マイナンバー is automatically assigned, and you can apply for a マイナンバーカード
- Foreign residents who have not applied for a マイナンバーカード can receive medical care normally using the **資格確認書**
- The integrated 在留カード and マイナンバーカード system (特定在留カード) was announced in June 2024; the implementation date is not yet determined, and obtaining it is **voluntary**, not mandatory

## Common Misconceptions Clarified

| Misconception | Correct Information |
|------|---------|
| "I can just bring my old insurance card to the hospital" | ❌ 旧保険証 became fully invalid on 2025.12.1 |
| "Without a マイナカード I can't use my insurance" | ❌ 資格確認書 allows normal insured medical care |
| "Having a マイナカード means I have an insurance card" | △ You also need to complete usage registration before it can be used as an insurance card |
| "If the card reader breaks, I have to pay the full amount" | ❌ It can be handled via visual confirmation mode or a 資格申立書 |
