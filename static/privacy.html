Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

NebulaInfinity ("we", "us") operates Japan Life Navigator ("the Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, nationality, residence status, and region (when you register).
  • Usage Data: AI chat history, saved tracker items, and language preferences.
  • Anonymous Users: For anonymous users, we store a device-generated identifier and optional profile preferences. No personal information is required.
  • Technical Data: We may collect anonymized analytics such as page views and error logs to improve the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the Service (e.g., AI responses tailored to your visa status and region).
  • To manage your account and credit balance.
  • To improve the Service's quality and accuracy.
  • To communicate with you about the Service when necessary.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. Account authentication is provided by Google Firebase. Chat history and profile data are stored on our servers. Tracker items are stored locally on your device only.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and user management. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • AI Providers: Chat messages are processed by third-party AI models. Messages are not used for model training.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with third-party services as described above, or when required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time via Settings.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Update your profile information at any time.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

9. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the Service.

11. Contact
